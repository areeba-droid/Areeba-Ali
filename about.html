About Me!

I am currently a third-year student at Simon Fraser University, completing a BA in the School of Interactive Arts and Technology, concentrating in Design. Growing up, I always pursued creative hobbies, which is why becoming a designer just felt right. I am a UX and visual designer, but I am always open to expanding my skills to other areas. I have always heard that design is communication, which is why I like to use design to express myself.

In my free time, I like to get involved in my community through volunteering. I am currently part of the Interactive Arts and Technology Student Union as the Treasurer, as well as the Finance manager for SIAT FROSH 2024. I also am a part of other smaller clubs on the side!

Outside of school, I run a (very) small jewelry business, where I can make pretty necklaces to scratch my creative itch. I love exploring different worlds through books and movies, and when I have time, I will try out a new (or old) baking recipe or pick up my crochet hook to start a new project!
